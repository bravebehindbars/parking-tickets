WHAT HAPPENS AFTER YOU GET TICKETS?

Your tickets start to pile up and then this causes life to get harder for you things like these happen

  • If you have multiple unpaid tickets and the issuing agency reports you to the DMV, your driver's license may be suspended. You may also face additional fees and the cost of reinstating your license.
  • If you have two or more unpaid parking tickets, your vehicle may be booted, even if you were legally parked at the time.
  • f you pay a ticket with a credit card and then reverse the payment, the ticket may be sent to collections and a default judgment may be entered against you. You may not be able to use a credit or debit card to make future payments.
